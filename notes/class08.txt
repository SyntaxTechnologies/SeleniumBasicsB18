WebTables:
Webtables are elements on the webpage, which are constructed by having
rows and columns

Types of webtables:
1. Static webtables
2. dynamic webtables

WebTables consist of defferent sections or parts:
<table>
        <thead>
                <tr>
                    <th></th> --> column data or heading
                </tr>
        </thead>
        <tbody>
                <tr>
                    <td></td> --> table data
                </tr>
        </tbody>
</table>
